Purpose of the Lab

The purpose of Lab Practice and Assignment 01 is to bring all students to a common baseline level of semantic HTML skills while following strict rules related to accessibility and structure.

This lab focuses on understanding and applying Human–Computer Interaction (HCI) models, specifically Norman’s Action Cycle, GOMS, and the Keystroke-Level Model (KLM).

Scope of Work

All content in this website is limited to the discussion of Norman’s Action Cycle, GOMS, and KLM. The site avoids the use of JavaScript and complex CSS to emphasize semantic structure and accessibility.

HCI Models Covered

  • Norman’s Seven Stages of Action
  • GOMS (Goals, Operators, Methods, Selection Rules)
  • KLM (Keystroke-Level Model and execution time prediction)
